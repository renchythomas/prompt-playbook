The Golden Formula

Great prompts give the AI a clear job. For the best results, try to include these 4 pieces in your request:

  • Role: Tell the AI who it is. ("Act as an expert chef...")
  • Task: State exactly what you need. ("Create a 3-day meal plan...")
  • Context: Provide background details. ("I only have chicken, rice, and veggies...")
  • Format: Tell it how you want the answer to look. ("Give it to me in a table...")
